Architrove Blog


In the dynamic landscape of technological progress, I, a former architecture student now immersed in design, have established Architrove as a sanctuary for infusing warmth and personal connection back into the realm of architecture. More than just a digital space, this blog serves as my endeavor to bridge the gap between the digital and the personal, focusing on bringing a sense of individuality to each house. The process involves sending meticulously crafted letters, unfolding an invitation to embark on an architectural journey. Architrove's spotlight is on the unique, diverse, and thoughtfully designed homes of the Bay Area, embracing all sizes and styles with a primary emphasis on the integrity and depth of thought behind the architecture. As the curator, I delve into these spaces, capturing their essence through photography and narrating their stories through intimate interviews with homeowners.

Architrove goes beyond a mere collection of visuals and words; it is an homage to the interplay between personal narratives and the spaces we inhabit, celebrating the nuances of architecture, the echoes of history in every brick, and the dreams resonating in each room. Join me in rediscovering the beauty of architecture, one invitation at a time.

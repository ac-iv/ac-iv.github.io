Send2Lino I.M.


In my broader initiative to infuse intimacy into the instant comforts of 21st-century living, this project becomes a conduit for messages, seamlessly guiding them to a receipt printer on my nightstand. This tangible addition imparts a pragmatic touch to the bedside, ready to translate digital communication into tangible records. It epitomizes the harmonious blend of modern ease and material dependability, offering a discreet yet utilitarian presence in the nocturnal sphere.

Our tactile experiences, often reduced to the cold touch of glass screens, inspire my projects to bridge the gap between diminished satisfaction and personalized services. Analog technologies serve as a crucial link, retaining the conveniences of smart-tech while fostering a more intimate and tactile connection with the digital world.

Welcome to the Product Design portion of my portfolio. Here, I have comprised my favorite projects (personal, educational, and freelance). Environmentally Aware Grocery Bags

The vision behind the Environmentally Aware Grocery Bag is to promote reusing plastic bags by gently reminding consumers that, when disposed, the bag will harm the earth.

Fortune Cookie Business Cards

Want to make an impact on a new connection? I introduce the Fortune Cookie Business Card. With profound and beautiful fortunes, your new connection will feel inclined to keep your contact information, and will associate good fortune and positivity with you every time they see it!

I Heart Myself Sticker

In a world where hatred seems to over saturate love, little notions of self love seem to have risen in popularity. This is my testament to self-love, a sticker which constantly reminds its owner that they love themselves.

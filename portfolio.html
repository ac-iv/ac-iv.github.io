Portfolio V1


In my initial foray into web development, I embarked on the creation of my first major HTML and CSS project – my very own portfolio website. Overflowing with enthusiasm and fueled by the desire to showcase my burgeoning design skills, I poured my creativity into every element of the site. The result was a visually striking and intricate design that, while a testament to my capabilities at the time, proved to be a bit overwhelming and lacked accessibility.

Proud of the accomplishment but recognizing the need for a more user-friendly and minimalistic approach, I eventually decided to embark on a redesign journey. The experience with my first portfolio site served as a valuable learning curve, prompting me to prioritize simplicity and accessibility in my subsequent web design endeavors.

Overview of Mission Bay Shuttle App Redesign

The Mission Bay Shuttle App redesign project aimed to enhance the user experience (UX) by addressing issues such as difficult navigation, inconsistent design, and poor accessibility. The goal was to create a seamless, intuitive, and enjoyable experience for the users of the shuttle service, allowing them to easily access schedules, routes, and other important information.

Details and Development

This project followed a structured design process involving research, wireframing, prototyping, and user testing. By analyzing user feedback and identifying pain points, the team worked to streamline navigation and create a cohesive design language throughout the app.

Key development highlights include:

  • User-Centered Design: Emphasis on improving usability and accessibility for a wide range of users.
  • Consistent Visual Language: A unified design system to ensure visual consistency and easy navigation.
  • Improved Navigation: Simplified routes and schedules, making key information easily accessible to users.

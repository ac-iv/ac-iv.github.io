Overview of Ketchup

Ketchup is a food-focused social media app that brings people together through shared culinary experiences. It allows users to discover and review restaurants, share photos, and engage with food trends, all in one elegant, user-friendly platform. Ketchup was designed to foster a community where food lovers can connect, recommend their favorite spots, and create visual stories around their dining experiences.

Details and Development

The app's clean and intuitive design ensures easy navigation, featuring a minimalist interface that highlights user-generated content without overwhelming visuals. Key development highlights include:

  • Responsive Design: Ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, whether on mobile or desktop.
  • Foodie Community: Building a space for food enthusiasts to share real-time reviews, photos, and stories with others who appreciate great food.
  • Custom App Icon: Developed a simple, clean icon that reflects the essence of food and community without cartoonish elements, enhancing the app's professional yet approachable brand identity.

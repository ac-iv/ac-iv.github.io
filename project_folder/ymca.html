YMCA Trophy Case Design

The YMCA Trophy Case Design project showcases the achievements of the YMCA community through a modern, functional display. The design emphasizes clean lines and durable materials to create a versatile space for awards and memorabilia, enhancing the visual appeal of the facility.

The trophy case acts as a centerpiece that fosters a sense of pride and unity within the YMCA community. It is designed for adaptability and longevity, making it a lasting testament to member achievements.

Locker Project Overview: Site Visits, 3D Scans, and SketchUp Conversion

Site Visit & Data Collection: Conducted site visits and used a handheld LiDAR scanner to capture the dimensions and spatial layout of the area designated for the trophy case.

Lobby Project Overview: Site Visits, 3D Scans, and SketchUp Conversion

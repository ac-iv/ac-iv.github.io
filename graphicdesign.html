The RealReal Web Banner Project

For a Visual Communication class I am taking this Fall (2023), I was tasked to create an array of web banners for a local San Francisco based company. The company I chose was The RealReal. Through the process of creating a variety of web banners for The RealReal, I gained valuable insights into the intricate balance between visual aesthetics and conveying the essence of a San Francisco-based company, fostering a deeper understanding of the complexities of visual communication in the realm of digital design.

This was the primary design for my Ad campaign. The RealReal is a Luxury reseller, and this Ad enhances the pact that "Luxury" means more than designer clothing.

This Ad is a collage style layout which is meant to capture a similar message as the previous Ad, that TRR is a consignment for anything luxury, more than just clothing.

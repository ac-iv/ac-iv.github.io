Don't worry dear, I am Immortal.


Embarking on a journey into the realm of font design, I became deeply engrossed in reflections on mortality and the peculiar realization that, despite the apparent ordinariness of creating a font, it was uniquely mine. To challenge these musings, I chose to craft a font that mimics handwriting, symbolically extending my existence beyond mortality by preserving my handwriting for posterity. This creative endeavor is encapsulated within a book, serving as a medium to explore the intertwined themes of creation and immortality. The book is aptly titled Don't Worry Dear, I am Immortal, featuring a poem I penned to illuminate this profound connection.

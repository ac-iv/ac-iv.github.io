Avelino J. Cortina IV

Avelino J. Cortina IV is a creative designer with a passion for UI/UX and architecture. Their approach to design focuses on blending innovative thinking with practical solutions, creating experiences that engage and inspire. From tech startups to community-driven projects, Avelino's work embodies a commitment to form, function, and a human-centered approach.

Based in San Francisco, Avelino combines a diverse background in design with a love for technology, constantly exploring new ways to push the boundaries of digital and physical spaces. Whether working on product designs, interactive experiences, or architectural concepts, they aim to make a meaningful impact through thoughtful, inclusive design.

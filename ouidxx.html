Mighty Leaf Tea Package Redesign

I decided to give Mighty Leaf tea a facelift because, honestly, the old packaging was a bit dull for such an exciting range of flavors. I played around with colors that capture the vibe of each blend, making the box pop with energy. And let's talk about those pouches – I wanted you to see the tea leaves in all their glory. This redesign isn't just a visual upgrade; it's my way of saying, 'Hey, let's make tea time an experience, not just a routine.'
